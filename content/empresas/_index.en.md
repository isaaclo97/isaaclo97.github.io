## Founded Companies

### [CiberScore](https://ciberscore.es) — University Spin-off · 2025

University spin-off (EBT) of the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos. Cybersecurity scoring and assessment platform for users and organisations, with training challenges and automated metrics.

---

### [YottaChess / YottaBase](https://yottachess.com) · 2021

The largest free online chess database in Spanish. Includes news, updated FIDE rankings, tournaments and game statistics. Exceeds **100,000 monthly visits**. Built with Python and Flask.

---

### [Muzpic Technology](https://muzpic.com) · 2022

A startup specialising in the creation of jewellery and visual artworks inspired by songs using artificial intelligence. Founded by Guillermo Martín (URJC), it develops a unique algorithm that transforms musical pieces into pictorial representations, combining art, technology and emotion.

During its time at the **Móstoles City Council Business Incubator**, Muzpic was recognised with the **Disruptive Technology Explorer Award** and secured a place in the **Banco Santander Explorer Programme**, which took its team to San Francisco alongside other selected entrepreneurs.

It has also created an app that allows users to scan the generated artworks to activate advertising content, opening new monetisation channels through digital art.

---

### [TestsConducir.es](https://testsconducir.es) · 2021

A preparation website for the driving theory exam, with up-to-date tests and accuracy statistics.
